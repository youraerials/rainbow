Take back your digital life.

Rainbow turns this Mac into your personal cloud — email, photos, files, documents, and media, hosted on hardware you own.

What this installer does

  • Drops Rainbow at /Applications/Rainbow/
  • Fetches the runtime tools (Apple Container, cloudflared, restic, yq, jq) directly from their official sources — no Homebrew, no Xcode required
  • Initializes the container runtime and pulls the Rainbow image (~50 MB) from our registry
  • Opens the setup wizard in your browser to finish: domain, services, administrator account

Requires macOS 26 (Tahoe) on Apple Silicon, 8 GB RAM, and 30 GB free disk, and an internet connection. Total install time: about five minutes once you start, plus a few minutes in the wizard.
