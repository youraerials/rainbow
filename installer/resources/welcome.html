Take back your digital life.

Rainbow turns this Mac into your personal cloud — email, photos, files, documents, and media, hosted on hardware you own.

What this installer does

  • Installs Homebrew + Apple Container runtime if they're not already here
  • Drops Rainbow at /Applications/Rainbow/
  • Pulls the container image and starts the setup wizard in your browser

Requires macOS 26 (Tahoe) on Apple Silicon, 8 GB RAM, and 30 GB free disk. The wizard finishes everything else — domain choice, services, administrator account.
