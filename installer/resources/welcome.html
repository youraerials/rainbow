Welcome to Rainbow

Rainbow turns your Mac Mini into a complete self-hosted platform: email, photos, files, documents, media, and more.

Take back your digital life. All your data stays on your hardware.

This installer will:

  • Install required dependencies (Homebrew, Apple Container, CLI tools)
  • Set up Stalwart mail server and Jellyfin media server
  • Configure container services (Photos, Files, Documents, Auth)
  • Launch the setup wizard for domain and service configuration

Requirements: Mac Mini M1+, macOS 26+, 16GB+ RAM, internet connection
