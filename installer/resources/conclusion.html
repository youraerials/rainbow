Almost there.

Rainbow is installed at /Applications/Rainbow/. Setup is still running in the background — fetching the Rainbow image and starting the wizard takes a moment. Your browser will open automatically when it's ready.

If your browser didn't open

The wizard URL is written to /Applications/Rainbow/.wizard-url when it's ready:

open "$(cat /Applications/Rainbow/.wizard-url)"

If something went wrong

The install log is at /tmp/rainbow-install.log. Setup runs as a LaunchAgent at rocks.rainbow.setup — you can re-trigger it with launchctl kickstart -k gui/$(id -u)/rocks.rainbow.setup.

Starting over

Double-click /Applications/Rainbow/Reset Rainbow.command to wipe everything (subdomain claim, containers, data volumes, Keychain) and start fresh.
